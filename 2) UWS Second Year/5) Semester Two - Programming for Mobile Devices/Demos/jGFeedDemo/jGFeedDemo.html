Demo of jQuery.jGfeed.js Library

App Details

This demo app shows how to use the the jquery.jgfeed library code to retrieve RSS feeds as well-defined objects. The app shows a number of different RSS feeds being accessed.

  • BBC Weather Feed
  • ScotRail Timetable/News Feed
  • BBC Football News Feed
  • Add your own RSS Feed
