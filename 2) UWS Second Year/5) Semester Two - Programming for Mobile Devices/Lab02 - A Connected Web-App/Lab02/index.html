This app uses the Fixer.io service to retrieve foreign exchange data.

The data accessed is accurate within a 24 hour period, but would not be suitable to provide a data feed for currency trading (services for that are, needless to say, expensive). Information provided in this app is for use only as a guide to foreign exchange rates. Any reliance you place on such information is therefore strictly at your own risk.
